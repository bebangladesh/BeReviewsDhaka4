About

Behance Bangladesh is a community of creative people which is approved and supported by Behance & Adobe. The goal of the community is to initiate online and offline activities like arranging events, connecting members and create better opportunities for creatives around the country.

Behance Portfolio Reviews, branded as BeReviews, is such a global community event hosted by local Behance communities around the world.
